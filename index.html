Frequently Asked Questions

How does Sondra's AI technology work?

Our AI continuously learns from new threat intelligence and adapts its scanning techniques to identify both known and zero-day vulnerabilities across your entire digital infrastructure.

What types of vulnerabilities can Sondra detect?

Sondra detects a wide range of vulnerabilities including SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, authentication bypass, misconfigurations, exposed APIs, weak encryption, and many more. Our platform covers OWASP Top 10 and beyond.

How often does Sondra scan my domains?

Sondra offers flexible scanning schedules from continuous monitoring to daily, weekly, or monthly scans based on your needs. Critical assets can be monitored in real-time while others can follow a scheduled approach.

Is Sondra suitable for small businesses?

Absolutely! Sondra is designed to scale from small businesses to enterprise organizations. Our pricing and feature tiers ensure that businesses of all sizes can benefit from enterprise-grade security scanning.

How quickly can I get started with Sondra?

Getting started is quick and easy. After booking a demo, our team will contact you as soon as possible. The initial setup involves adding your domains, and our AI will begin scanning immediately.
